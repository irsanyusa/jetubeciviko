UFC 296: Edwards vs. Covington features a UFC Welterweight Championship bout between current champ Leon Edwards and former interim champion Colby Covington. Edwards captured the title after taking down Kamaru Usman. Covington, meantime, returns to the octagon for the first time since March 2022.

The UFC 296 main card also features a UFC Flyweight Championship bout between current champion Alexandre Pantoja vs. Brandon Royval.

Other matches on the main fight card include Shavkat Rakhmonov vs. Stephen Thompson; Tony Ferguson vs. Paddy Pimblett; and an all-American tilt between Josh Emmett and Bryce Mitchell.
