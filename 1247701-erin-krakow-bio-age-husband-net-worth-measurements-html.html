Erin Krakow's bio

Erin Krakow was born to Dr. Elliott and Morgan Krakow on September 5, 1984, in Philadelphia, USA. She grew up in Wellington, Florida, alongside her younger brother named, Kyle.

Education

Erin went to the Alexander W. Dreyfoos Jr. School of the Arts, where she honed her acting skills. She then joined New York City's Juilliard School and graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts.

Career

Krakow began her acting career in school when she appeared in school plays like George the Fourth and The Importance of Being Earnest.

Her first television role came in 2010 when she got the role of Tanya Gabriel in Army Wives. In 2013, she got a guest role in Castle, followed by a starring role in Chance at Romance.
