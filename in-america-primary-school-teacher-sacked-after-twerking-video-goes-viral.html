Primary school teacher sacked after twerking video goes viral

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 14, 2024

The 24-year-old teacher, Miss Clarissa, who had been on holiday in Cabo, San Lucas when the footage had been recorded, reveals that she had been forced to quit her job after angry parents had found out about the video clip.

Miss Clarissa had taught in the northern Mexican state of Sonora for three years prior to her dismissal.

While speaking to Mexican media, Miss Clarissa said:

“They didn’t give me any other option at my school than to resign.

"I didn’t know what else to do apart from leave as quickly as I could.

“It was a dance competition, something like this doesn’t define me as a person, it was my free time and we’re in the 21st century."

Miss Clarissa had also reportedly claimed the first price for the dance competition she had participated in which had led to the lose of her job.

“I knew there were mobile phones but I never imagined this would go viral and anyway, I’m not doing anything bad."

The disgraced teacher might still be in luck as reports have revealed that a backlash has begun on the internet with close to 150 people signing a change.org petition which allegedly calls for the immediate reinstatement of Miss Clarissa.

An accompanying text, which is alleged to have been written by parents who did not support her "sacking", has also been released, threatening a school fees boycott if Miss Clarissa wasn’t reinstated.
