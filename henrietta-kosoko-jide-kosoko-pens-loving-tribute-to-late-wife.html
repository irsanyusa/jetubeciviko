Jide Kosoko pens loving tribute to late wife

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 12, 2024

The late actress was buried in her hometown on, Saturday,June 10, 2016 and her husband, actor, Jide Kosoko penned a moving tribute to her.

He wrote, “I find it disheartening that my treasured wife passed away at R-Jolad hospital. It was a terrible shock because the sickness was so brief and never looked like a terminal one.

ADVERTISEMENT

My darling Henrietta was not only a wonderful wife and mother but she was my meticulous partner in my profession as well as housekeeper and homemaker. She was a cheerful, friendly person and made many warm friends.

Rita seldom, if ever criticized any person. Instead, she preferred to refer to their good qualities. My wife was the soul of honesty. She would never shade the truth no matter what the consequences.

It is difficult for me to estimate Rita. She departs this sinful word with a substantial proportion of me. We did not only fall in love but also built our relationship and were also fond of ourselves till death.

She inspired me in ways I cannot explain. I have lost a worthy helpmate but I have nothing but happy memories of her in this world.

ADVERTISEMENT

Adieu Henrietta Kosoko. I sorrow now for my beloved wife but the burden has been lessened by the many kindness shown my family and me.”

The late actress who was battling with diabetes, slumped at her residence and was rushed down to the hospital, then taken back home where died on Monday, June 6, 2016.
