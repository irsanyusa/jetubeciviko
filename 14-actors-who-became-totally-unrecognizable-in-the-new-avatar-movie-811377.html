14 Actors Who Became Totally Unrecognizable in the New Avatar Movie

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 26, 2024

The audience had to wait for the release of the new Avatar movie for a long 13 years. The main characters of the film are the people of Na’vi who live on the planet Pandora. And they look super-realistic thanks to motion capture technology and the famous actors who play them. We decided to compare the photos of real actors and actresses and their characters in the movie Avatar: The Way of Water, and we were struck by how different they look (though some facial features are still recognizable).

1. Sam Worthington as Jake Sully, a former human, who became the chief of the Omatikaya

Walt Disney Co./Courtesy Everett Collection/East News, ISABEL INFANTES/AFP/East News

2. Zoe Saldaña as Neytiri, the wife of the chief of the Omatikaya

Walt Disney Co./Courtesy Everett Collection/East News, ISABEL INFANTES/AFP/East News

3. CCH Pounder as Mo’at, the Omatikaya’s Tsahìk, and Neytiri’s mother

4. Sigourney Weaver as Kiri, the adopted daughter of Jake and Neytiri

20th Century Studios/Associated Press/East News, ANTHONY WALLACE/AFP/East News

5. Jamie Flatters as Neteyam, Jake and Neytiri’s eldest son

6. Britain Dalton as Lo’ak, Jake and Neytiri’s youngest son

7. Trinity Jo-Li Bliss as Tuktirey, Jake and Neytiri’s daughter

Walt Disney Co./Courtesy Everett Collection/East News, Scott Garfitt/Invision/East News

8. Stephen Lang as Colonel Miles Quaritch

Walt Disney Co./Courtesy Everett Collection/East News, MICHAEL TRAN/AFP/East News

9. Jack Champion as Miles “Spider” Socorro, a young man who was born on Pandora

Walt Disney Co./Courtesy Everett Collection/East News, ISABEL INFANTES/AFP/East News

10. Duane Evans, Jr. as Rotxo, a young male hunter and free-diver of the Metkayina

11. Cliff Curtis as Tonowari, the chief of the Metkayina clan

Walt Disney Co./Courtesy Everett Collection/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

12. Kate Winslet as Ronal, Tonowari’s wife

Walt Disney Co./Courtesy Everett Collection/East News, Scott Garfitt/Invision/East News

13. Bailey Bass as Tsireya, a free diver of the Metkayina and Tonowari’s daughter

20th Century Studios/Associated Press/East News, Scott Garfitt/Invision/East News

14. Filip Geljo as Aonung, a young male hunter of the Metkayina and Tonowari’s son
