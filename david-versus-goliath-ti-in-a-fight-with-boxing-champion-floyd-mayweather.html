T.I In A Fight With Boxing Champion Floyd Mayweather

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

According to reports, the rapper got into a fight with Floyd because T.I.’s wife, Tameka “Tiny” Cottle posted a picture on Instagram with Floyd’s daughter, Iyanna Mayweather.

Sources have it that T.I  got angry after his wife posted a pic on Instagram with Money’s daughter, with the caption, “Had to come celebrate with my new boo @moneyyaya Happy Birthday bae.” Feeling that Floyd was trying to get closer to his wife, T.I went to confront the boxer about it, but flew into a rage when Floyd referred to Tiny using the words, “control your bi— motherf—–.”

Reports have it that the  bodyguards stepped in to separate the fight before any serious exchange of blows ensued. A fat Burger employee didn't escape unscathed though, as he was slashed during the foray.

It  was reported that no arrest was made as the Las Vegas Police Department arrived the scene just as Floyd was driving out. T.I on his part had already left.

Sources have it that Floyd is not happy that T.I. would think that, when they’re all friendly, especially because it was Tiny’s idea to hang out at Las Vegas in the first place.
