Katy Perry is Reportedly Marrying Orlando Bloom on Valentines Day 2024

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 24, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

Photo by Michael Kovac/Getty Images for Lifetime

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Katy Perry and Orlando bloom may be celebrating Valentine’s Day by tying the knot. The pop megastar and actor are reportedly planning to get married in February, five years after Bloom popped the question to the American Idol judge.

Katy Perry, Orlando Bloom to Marry on Valentine’s Day

Since becoming an item in 2016, the couple has marked several relationship milestones together and even overcome rough patches along the process. Now, Perry and Orlando see 2024 as the perfect year to tie the knot, a source from Heat reported.

“Katy and Orlando have postponed their wedding multiple times, but 2024 is the year they want to make it happen,” the insider said. “They’re happier than ever and everyone is predicting they’ll get married soon – possibly even on Valentine’s Day, which is the anniversary of their engagement.”

It’s true that Perry’s 2023 left her jam-packed, having dealt with American Idol and her Las Vegas residency. Nonetheless, the insider said the singer and Bloom didn’t leave their wedding plans behind. The lovely pair apparently considered marrying in Japan, but they realized the logistics would be difficult.

“Doing something closer to home in California makes a lot more sense. Their place in Montecito has nine acres, so there’s plenty of room to do it there. Plus, it’s close to LA, so all of their showbiz friends could easily attend,” the source said.

Perry and Bloom initially met in 2016 during the Golden Globe Awards. They then dated for less than a year before breaking up in 2017. Despite split though, the pair remained friends until they got together in 2018.

On Valentine’s Day 2019, the Pirates of the Caribbean actor proposed to the “Roar” hitmaker. Over a year later, the couple welcomed their first child together, Daisy Dove Bloom.

The Couple is Allegedly Trying For Another Baby

According to the same source, the couple is also eyeing to have another baby this year. With that plan at play, it’s thus possible that Perry will be a “pregnant bride.”

“It’s no secret that she very much wants to have baby number two, and this year really makes the most sense. She’s finished her big Las Vegas residency, so she’s got some time in her schedule,” the source said. “Plus, at three, Daisy is old enough now to understand the whole thing. She’s ready to be a big sister, too.”

Seemingly, the two have envisioned 2024 to be the year to further strengthen their family bond. Lately, Bloom and Perry had reportedly been enjoying lots of romantic dates.

“Having Daisy has totally united them – they’re more in love now than they’ve ever been,” the source said. “At this point, Katy’s staying pretty tight-lipped about their plans, but her friends are all saying this will be the year they make it official.”
