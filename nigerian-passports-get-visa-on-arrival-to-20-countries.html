Nigerian passports get visa on arrival to 20 countries

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 30, 2024

The Nigerian passport gets visa-on-arrival access to territories in Oceania, the Middle East, the Caribbean, Asia and Africa.

The Henley Passport Index is clear that the Nigerian passport is one of the weakest in the world, going by its ranking of the first quarter of 2023.
