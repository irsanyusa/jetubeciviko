Lindsay Lohans Ex, Samantha Ronson, Shares Her Thoughts on the Pregnancy News

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 9, 2024

Lindsay Lohan just confirmed her pregnancy with fiancé Bader Shammas, to whom she has been linked since 2020. Lohan, however, has received a great deal of media attention for her previous relationships, particularly the one with Samantha Ronson. Although their brief but intense romance ended amicably, Ronson came out and shared her feelings regarding her ex-girlfriend’s pregnancy.

Lindsay Lohan announced the upcoming birth of her first child. She captioned an image of a white onesie with the words “coming soon” and said, “We are blessed and joyful.”

This is a watershed moment for Lohan who has continually drawn much media attention for her controversies. It was difficult for her to rebuild her life since she had to confront all the prejudices and assumptions about her reputation. Even so, her life has changed over the last several years, with her bravely re-entering the public eye and being blessed with the love of a man and an upcoming baby.

Lohan has found her other half in Bader, the man who has accepted her beyond any boundaries or expectations. Thus, the actress and model has fallen head over heels for him after he came into her life unexpectedly. “You never know if you’re going to find that in life, and he’s an amazing guy, man. I love him, and we’re a great team,” gushed the actress.

The couple secretly married in July 2022, and they now live in Dubai. “I have an amazing husband who is very calm. Just the best. And my family,” Lohan said.

Lindsay Lohan and Samantha Ronson had a high-profile, on-again, off-again romantic relationship that lasted for several years in the late 2000s. The couple reportedly began dating in 2008 and were often seen together in public, sparking media attention and speculation about their relationship.

It all started off as a friendship before developing into something romantic. Lohan hinted about her relationship with Ronson without confirming it outright. But she later claimed, “I think it’s pretty obvious who I’m seeing. I think it’s no shock to anyone that it’s been going on for quite some time. She’s a wonderful person, and I love her very much.”

KCS PRESSE/EAST NEWS

Lohan and Ronson started dating when it was still uncommon to see same-sex relationships in the entertainment industry. However, their relationship was also marked by much controversy and drama. “I was bold enough to say, ’Yeah, I like a girl. And?’” Lohan admitted. “I actually was kind of the first young person straight off the Disney train, so it was a big deal. My younger siblings didn’t know what to make of it.”

The couple did, however, formally end their relationship in 2009. After a while, Lohan addressed the breakup, saying, “If I weren’t with Samantha, I would probably be with a boy next. She’s the only woman I’ve been attracted to.”

FZS/SIPA/EAST NEWS

After their breakup, Lohan and Ronson remained friends and occasionally appeared together.
“I needed to love myself first before I could be with anyone,” the actress said. “And I was going through a lot. Lohan added, “We’re friends now. That’s how it started, so I think that’s what’s meant to be.”

Yet, years later, Lohan addressed their relationship again, making it clear she had a more friendly than loving relationship with Ronson. “We were always more like best friends as well.”

After Lohan shared the happy news of her pregnancy, many people left comments on her post, including stars like Amanda Seyfried, Bella Thorne, and Paris Hilton, who wrote, “Congratulations, love! So happy for you. Welcome to the Mommy Club!”

Like the rest, Ronson was concerned about conveying her thoughts on the news. But Ronson only had good things to say about her ex-girlfriend Lohan, who is expecting her first child. “I wish her all the best. It’s always great news when someone is expecting a baby,” the DJ shared.

Nevertheless, while love fades, so does resentment, and even after many years, Ronson and Lohan still care for each other, always being present for special moments like this one.
