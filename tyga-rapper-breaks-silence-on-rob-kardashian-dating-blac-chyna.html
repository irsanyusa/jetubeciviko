Rapper breaks silence on Rob Kardashian dating Blac Chyna

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, June 21, 2024

E! News reports that the 26-year-old rapper has giving his blessings to the new Hollywood couple. When asked about the Rob/Blac affair, he replied, "Everyone deserves to be happy."

A source told TMZ.com: "Khloe became enraged and asked Chyna, 'What the f*** are you doing in my house?' The two bolted into Rob's wing and locked the door. Khloé followed suit and told them both to get out. Khloé made it clear to Rob, he was no longer welcome in her home."

The reality TV star believed she had no choice but to chuck out Rob, who has become reclusive over the past 24 months, to teach him a lesson out of fear he was falling off the wagon again and Blac was preying on his weakness. The insider said: "Rob's sister was especially indignant when she learned Rob had purchased some expensive items for Blac as he doesn't have a lot of money to burn."

Rob, 28, is now believed to be living with Blac and she's been encouraging him to get in shape after introducing him to her personal trainer.
