20Deep Illustrations Revealing the Harsh Truth About the World Today

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Artists often use their canvas as a portal to express how they feel about people, places, and things. Lately, they have taken to satirical illustrations to show that the world we are living in today is a place where all that glitters, isn’t really gold and there’s a story behind every image. Society is ridden with unforeseen evils in various scenarios and it’s time the world sat up and took note of it. While all of this isn’t a pretty sight to see, it does make for some truly hard hitting and sometimes, disturbing art.

At Bright Side, we’ve compiled a list of illustrations from different artists around the world, who reveal this reality, and presented it here.

1. Give us this day our daily browsing limit.

2. Modern love keeps us connected, yet it keeps us apart.

3. Social media feeds narcissism.

4. It’s still a man’s world, sweetie.

5. Why don’t we care?

6. When your job turns into a nightmare:

7. Every child deserves a childhood.

8. Extra-marital affairs ruin families.

9. Life comes full circle.

10. Will any of this matter one day?

11. What do you see?

12. There sure is nothing scary.

13. It’s all about the money, honey.

14. Every child needs his family.

15. The harsh reality of child labor

16. Could this happen to us one day?

17. When will women ever get equality?

18. The roots of addiction

19. The pressure to post pictures of a perfect life

20. The race never ends, does it?

Which illustration hit your soul the hardest? Share your opinion with us in the comments.
