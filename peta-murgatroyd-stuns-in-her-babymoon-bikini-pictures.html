Peta Murgatroyd Stuns in Her Babymoon Bikini Pictures

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 11, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Peta Murgatroyd via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Two months before her due date, pregnant Peta Murgatroyd made sure to flex her baby bump while wearing bikinis. The Dancing With The Stars pro has recently spent a tropical babymoon with her husband Maks Chmerkovskiy, alongside their six-year-old son Shai.

Peta Murgatroyd Displays Pregnancy Bump Amid Their Babymoon

The married couple headed to Cabo San Lucas for a lush babymoon at the beach, three months after their second pregnancy announcement. Through a series of photos on Instagram, Murgatroyd updated her followers about her baby bump. The DWTS pro was evidently enjoying the quick vacation, as seen in her confident bikini snaps online.

“Paradise with my baby,” she wrote as caption.

In the pictures, Murgatroyd was wearing a grayish white bikini, paired with a pair of chunky specs. Another photo also showed the DWTS pro sporting a beige, long sleeve dress with a cut-out. She completed this outfit by wearing gold hoops and a necklace to match the same. Wearing a simple yet sleek makeup, Murgatroyd definitely rocked her beach pics during such babymoon.

Their Son Shai Joined Them During the Outing

While babymoon usually involves just the couple, the DWTS pair travelled as a family of three. Murgatroyd and Chmerkovskiy had a cute tag-along in the flesh of their first child, Shai.

“Last vacay trip as a trio and we had an absolutely amazing time! Can’t wait to be back with our newest Chmerkovskiy,” he wrote.

Despite this unusual celebratory vacation setup, the couple obviously couldn’t be happier. The father and son, as seen on Instagram, have adorably played together, with Chmerkovskiy goofily burying Shai in the sand.

For those who may not know, the couple has experienced a couple of fertility issues in the past. Particularly, they suffered from three miscarriages and a failed embryo transfer. In the past though, Murgatroyd claimed that her husband has remained hopeful despite their pregnancy disappointments.

“Maks just truly believed this would happen how it did,” Murgatroyd said “He was always like, ‘You’re just going to get pregnant when you’re not stressed, when you’re happy, when everything is good.'”

Murgatroyd and Chmerkovskiy’s second baby will arrive in June.
