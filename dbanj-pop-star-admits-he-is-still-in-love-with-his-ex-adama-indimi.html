Pop star admits he is still in love with his ex Adama Indimi

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 12, 2024

That's normal between two former lovers, but D'banj's words in his birthday message already have a few people talking. In the caption of the picture D'banj more or less admitted that he is still in love with his former girlfriend who is the daughter of Nigerian billionaire Alhaji Mohammed Indimi.

In the caption of the picture he wrote "Happy Birthday to this very beautiful and extraordinary woman, She's 1 in a life time..my best friend, my closest and best ally, my beautiful princess the Queen of my heart have a blast my dear. I love you very much babe. Muah!"

In June, Adama Indimi was appointed as a Director of Grassroots Mobilizers for the President. After her appointment D'banj congratulated her on Instagram.

D'banj dated Adama Indimi circa 2012, but they split because of pressure from Adama's family. In related news Bonang Matheba was reported to have bought a Maybach.
