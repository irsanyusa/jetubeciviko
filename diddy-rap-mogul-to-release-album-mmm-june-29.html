Five years since his last studio album ‘Last Train to Paris’, it appears the founder of Bad Boy is ready to make a return in the form of the 'MMM' album.

Diddy posted two messages on his Instagram page referring to MMM and the release date (June 29).

One post was a photo of Kobe Bryant and Michael Jordan with the caption “#Fact #MMM June 29th 2015.” The second post was a video featuring a Diddy verse playing in the background.

ADVERTISEMENT

Diddy has released five studio albums since 1997 including his multi-platinum selling debut . Diddy also teamed with DJ/producer Guy Gerber for the 2014 side project ‘1111’.
