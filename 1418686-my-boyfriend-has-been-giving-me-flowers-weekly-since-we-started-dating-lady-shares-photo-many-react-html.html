A young lady identified on Twitter as 'Her' with the handle, @thealyseballard, has amazed thousands of social media users with a post of her bragging about her man.

"Lmfaoooo this how he feel bout going viral rn...oh yeah he cooks too. mr too good to be true."

He always shows love

Interestingly, on one occasion that the loving man failed to present a flower in an entire week, he made up for that with two in the following week.

The post went so viral that it got over 250,000 reactions and more than 1,400 comments at the time of this report.
