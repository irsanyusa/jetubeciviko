Roone Arledge (Sports Broadcasting Pioneer)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Profession: Sports Broadcasting Pioneer

Biography: President of ABC Sports from 1968 to 1986 and ABC News from 1977 until 1998, Arledge was a key part of the company's rise to equal status with the other two main US television networks, NBC and CBS.

Arledge created many programs still airing today, including "Monday Night Football", "20/20" and "ABC World New Tonight".

Born: July 8, 1931
Birthplace: New York City, New York, USA

Generation: Silent Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Goat/Sheep
Star Sign: Cancer

Died: December 5, 2002 (aged 71)
Cause of Death: Prostate cancer
